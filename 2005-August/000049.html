Just wanted to send out a quick status report.  I just recently
started to continue work on 3.x and now those changes are in

out the berlios subversion repository 3.x is leaps and bounds ahead of
2.x already.  3.x mainly features a better interface with drag and

currently looking for people to write window manager modules, test
